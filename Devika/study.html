Steps to study effectively

  • Set a fixed time to star studying everyday
  • Gather all books,notes,and stationery before sitting down
  • Turn off phone notifications to avoid distractions
  • Read the topic once to get an overview
  • Write down keypoints and important formulaes
  • Solve practice problems or exercises
  • Review your notes after completing the topic
  • Take a short 10-minute break after every hour
  • Revise the days topics before going to sleep
  • Repeat the process the next day with a new topic
